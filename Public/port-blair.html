Port Blair

Port Blair, the capital of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, is a vibrant destination in the Bay of Bengal. Known for its historical significance and natural beauty, it offers a blend of colonial heritage and tropical charm. Key attractions include the Cellular Jail, a historic prison-turned-museum, and the stunning beaches of Corbyn's Cove and Radhanagar. Port Blair serves as a gateway to explore the pristine islands and coral reefs of the archipelago, making it a popular base for tourists seeking adventure and relaxation in a tropical paradise.
